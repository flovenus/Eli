The North American Moose
This animal is awesome. It can get 2 meter tall shoulder height and has only the american Grizzly as a natural enemy. The north american moose is actually much taller than its european relative, the scandinavian moose. While the scandinavian moose gets to 1.60 to 1.70 m tall, the alaskan one is the biggest land mammal in north America.
